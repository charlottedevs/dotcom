About Us

We are a group specifically for junior developers that are looking to grow their skills and get connected with the Charlotte developer community. Our goal is to form a community that has a support network that helps people become more valuable to the market and land awesome jobs!

There is some financial risk that companies take on by interviewing and hiring a junior developer. Quite often you see "years of experience" listed on job requirements to mitigate that risk. Another goal is to get to know you and vouch for you based on your credibility, not how long you've been programming, so you can get that interview.

Interested in software development but not sure where to start? Just graduated from a programming boot camp? Started your first software development job and want to upgrade your skills? Our meetups will have fun challenges and some real world problems that more senior developers will help walk you through. Learn how to approach problems and use best practices.
